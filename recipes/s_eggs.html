Scrambled Eggs

Quick and easy egg recipe

A dish made from eggs stirred, whipped, or beaten together typically with salt, butter, oil, and sometimes other ingredients, and heated so that they form into curds.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs, milk and salt
  2. Heat pan, add butter and let it melt
  3. Pour eggs mixture and let it sit for around 20 seconds
  4. Lifting and fold mixture over from the bottom of the pan.
  5. Let it sit for 10 seconds
  6. Repeat until eggs are set
  7. Remove from heat and leave for a moment to finish cooking
