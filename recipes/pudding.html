Lemon Pudding

Very easy microwave recipe for lemon pudding

Make a lemon pudding in 10 minutes by using only your microwave!

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Lemon Zest
  • Lemond Curd

Steps

  1. Mix the sugar, butter, flour, eggs, lemon zest and vanilla together until creamy
  2. Microwave on High for 3 mins, turning halfway through cooking, until risen and set all the way through
  3. Heat the lemon curd for 30 secs in the microwave and stir until smooth
  4. Pour all over the top of the pudding and serve
