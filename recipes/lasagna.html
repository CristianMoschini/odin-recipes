Lasagna

An Italian dish made of stacked layers of pasta sheets

Layers of pasta alternating with fillings such as ragù (ground meats and tomato sauce), béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses (which may include ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan), seasonings and spices.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Ragù
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 180C and lightly oil an ovenproof dish
  2. Alternate pasta layers, ragù and bechamel until you have 3 layers of pasta
  3. Cover dish with the remaining bechamel
  4. Bake for about 40 mins until the top is browned
